Jalapeno Popper Dip

Serves 4-6

Ingredients:

  1. 1 8oz bloack cream cheese, softened
  2. 1/3 cup mayo
  3. 1/3 cup sour cream
  4. 1 tsp garlic powder
  5. 10 slices chopped cooked bacon
  6. 2 jalapenos, minced
  7. 1 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  8. 1 1/2 cup shredded moneterey jack cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350º.
  2. In a large bowl, stir together: cream cheese, mayo, sour cream
  3. Next, add garlic powder, most of the cooked bacon (reserve some for topping), most of the jalapeños (reserve some for topping)
  4. Lastly, add 1 cup cheddar, and 1 cup Monterey Jack and season with salt and pepper.

Transfer to a skillet for baking and sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cups cheddar and Monterey Jack, bacon, and jalapeños.

Bake until bubbly, 15 minutes. (If desired, broil 3 minutes.)
